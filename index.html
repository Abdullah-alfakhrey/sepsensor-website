The solution

Bedside. Automated. 10 minutes.

A compact optical reader and disposable cartridge. Insert, wait, read.

Self-calibrating optical readout

Accurate, repeatable results without external calibration equipment or trained lab staff.

Disposable microfluidic cartridge

Single-use, fully automated — designed for bedside nursing staff.

Continuous patient monitoring

Repeat measurements every 10 minutes — track IL-6 trends in real time.

Signal processing
